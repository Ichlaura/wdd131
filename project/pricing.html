Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), bank transfers, PayPal, and in some locations, cash payments.

Are there any discounts available?

Yes! We offer 10% discount for students who pay for 3 months in advance, and 15% discount for 6-month packages. We also have family discounts and referral bonuses.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

We offer a full refund if you cancel within the first week of classes. After that, refunds are prorated based on classes taken.
