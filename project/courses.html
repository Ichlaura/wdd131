English Level Guide

Beginner (A1)

Can understand and use familiar everyday expressions and very basic phrases.

Elementary (A2)

Can communicate in simple and routine tasks requiring a simple exchange of information.

Intermediate (B1)

Can deal with most situations likely to arise while traveling in an English-speaking area.

Upper Intermediate (B2)

Can interact with a degree of fluency and spontaneity that makes regular interaction possible.

Advanced (C1)

Can express ideas fluently and spontaneously without much obvious searching for expressions.

Proficiency (C2)

Can understand with ease virtually everything heard or read.
